About Kili-Arava

Kili‑Arava was founded in Kilimanjaro, Tanzania, by Brian Shoo, Edwin Moshi, and Ariel from Israel, Arava. Blending diverse perspectives, we invest across high‑impact sectors, from microfinance to technology, to drive sustainable growth.

Mission

Empower Tanzania's economic future through profitable, ethical, impactful investments.

Vision

To be Africa’s most respected and forward-thinking investment powerhouse.

Values

Integrity • Excellence • Sustainability
